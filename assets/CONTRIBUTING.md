# CONTRIBUTING.md

## Contributing to THE HOOK™

Thank you for your interest.

This is a **public, sanitized, defensive** repository. Contributions are reviewed selectively and must align with the repository's public-safety, legal, and product-positioning requirements.

---

## What Contributions Are Welcome

High-value contribution areas include:

- documentation clarity
- proofreading and structure improvements
- UX/UI refinement suggestions
- safe dashboard concepts
- architecture visualization improvements
- synthetic telemetry examples
- public demo polish
- repository hygiene and maintainability improvements

---

## What Will Not Be Accepted

Contributions will be rejected if they include or materially enable:

- offensive security workflows
- unauthorized targeting guidance
- credential abuse patterns
- exploit logic or weaponization
- traffic interception or manipulation methods
- ambiguous dual-use deployment instructions
- removal of legal ownership notices
- branding misuse or derivative commercialization attempts

---

## Contribution Rules

Before submitting changes:

1. Keep the repository's framing defensive and enterprise-safe.
2. Prefer clarity, restraint, and professionalism over sensational language.
3. Do not introduce restricted or sensitive implementation detail.
4. Preserve all legal notices and proprietary ownership statements.
5. Keep examples synthetic, non-operational, and safe for public review.

---

## Pull Request Guidance

A strong contribution should include:

- a clear rationale
- a concise summary of changes
- screenshots if UI-related
- confirmation that no secrets were added
- confirmation that the content remains public-safe

The maintainer may edit, decline, or close submissions at sole discretion.

---

## Issues and Suggestions

Use issues for:

- documentation corrections
- structure suggestions
- public-safe design feedback
- typo fixes
- UX observations

Do not use issues to request private enterprise materials or restricted implementation details.

---

## Intellectual Property

By contributing, you acknowledge that this repository is proprietary and that submission does not override repository ownership.
Any accepted contribution may be edited, reformatted, or integrated into the project by the maintainer.
